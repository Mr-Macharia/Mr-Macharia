## 🌟 About Me

> **"Empowering the next generation of tech leaders through mentorship and innovative AI solutions."**

I'm a passionate **AI Engineer** and **Tech Mentor** dedicated to solving real-world problems through cutting-edge technology. Currently building **Tech Hut Mentorship** - a revolutionary platform that connects youth with experienced mentors to accelerate their tech careers using AI-driven matching and personalized learning paths.

- 🔭 **Currently Working On**: Tech Hut Mentorship Platform
- 🌱 **Learning**: Advanced AI Engineering, MLOps, and Cloud Architecture
- 👯 **Looking to Collaborate**: AI/ML projects, EdTech solutions, and social impact initiatives
- 💡 **Goal**: Bridge the tech skills gap for African youth through accessible mentorship
- ⚡ **Fun Fact**: I believe code is poetry - expressing complex ideas simply and elegantly
